About Material Design

Material Design is Google's design language, combining bold colors, meaningful motion, and depth to create intuitive, beautiful user interfaces.

History

Created in 2014, Material Design evolved to unify mobile, web, and native apps with consistent design patterns.

Components

Cards, buttons, FABs, app bars, and other components follow a unified design system.

Guidelines

Material Design provides a detailed color system, typography rules, and motion principles to build modern apps.
