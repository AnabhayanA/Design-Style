Learn Material Design Principles

Material Design is Google's design system emphasizing hierarchy, elevation, motion, and bold color palettes.

Grid System

Everything aligns to an 8dp baseline grid for consistency and visual harmony.

Typography

Roboto font family ensures readability, hierarchy, and consistency across all components.

Colors

Vibrant, saturated primary and secondary colors create contrast and visual delight.

Motion & Interaction

Smooth animations and hover effects reveal relationships and guide users.

Elevation

Shadows create depth and hierarchy, distinguishing surfaces and elements.

Components

Buttons, cards, and FABs follow Material specifications for interaction and style.
