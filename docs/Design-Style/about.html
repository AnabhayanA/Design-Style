About Material Design

Material Design is Google's design language that combines Swiss grid systems with tactile surfaces, bold colors, and motion.

History

  • 2014: Introduced by Google
  • 2018: Material Design 2 with theming system
  • 2021: Material Design 3 with dynamic color and accessibility focus

Key Principles

  1. Elevation and shadows
  2. 8dp grid system
  3. Bold typography and color
  4. Motion and animation
