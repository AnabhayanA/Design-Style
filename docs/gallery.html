How to add your style site

  1. Copy `style-template.html` to a new folder under `docs/` (for example `docs/swiss/index.html`).
  2. Replace the placeholder research text with your historical summary, citations, and a short bibliography.
  3. Build a small demo section that shows 2–4 components demonstrating the style (use existing CSS utility classes where helpful).
  4. Commit your new folder and send the gallery URL (GitHub Pages or raw link) as your assignment submission.

Grading checklist

  • Contains a research/history section with at least 3 references.
  • Contains a demo section with visible examples (images or live components).
  • Uses accessible markup (roles/aria where relevant) and respects reduced-motion.
  • Page is reachable from this gallery (add a link here after creating your page).
